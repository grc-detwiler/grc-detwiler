Introduction

Margin \\ the publication for imperfect minimalists

Margins allow us to focus on what is important. Here at Margin, we prioritize white space in our environments and in our schedules. Our goal is to provide inspiration to the imperfect minimalist. We say no to the anxiety of perpetual consumerism and yes to the peace of simplicity.
